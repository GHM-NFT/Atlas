# GHM Atlas Command Centre Map v21

A controlled hybrid of the v19 Bird’s-eye Map and the v20 complete-data experience.

## Applied decisions

- Overview is the first left-navigation action.
- The map-view group remains directly beneath Overview.
- Timeline, Register, Dependencies, Evidence and Decisions form a separate project-navigation group.
- The official small gold workstream icons form a compact territory launcher in the left rail.
- The supplied Atlas Command Centre emblem is the Overview hero and the centre of the Bird’s-eye Map.
- Ethos Option 2 is used as the compact Overview banner.
- Truth, Legacy, Creativity, Integrity and Connection form a persistent bottom navigation dock.
- Bird’s-eye Map is a full-stage experience with coloured dependency dots, risk rings, layout controls and all 248 items.
- The permanent right panel has been removed.
- Workstream selection opens a translucent command chamber from right to left.
- The chamber closes on navigation, close button, background click or Escape.
- Waffle and Block maps use the complete dataset, official workstream icons, status colours and item detail interactions.
- All 248 prototype records are populated with owners, reviewers, statuses, risks, milestones, evidence, decisions and dependencies.
